About Us
This Los Angeles motel features an outdoor pool and is within 5 minutes' walk from CBS Television City, the Original Farmers Market and the Grove shopping center. It offers contemporary rooms with a microwave and refrigerator.

Each room at Beverly Laurel Motor Hotel is equipped with a work desk and cable TV. The tiled bathrooms feature a spacious glass-enclosed shower.

Beverly Laurel offers tours/ticket assistance and a 24-hour front desk.

The popular Swingers coffee shop and café is right next to this motel. Beverly Laurel Hotel is within 1 miles of the Los Angeles County Museum of Art and Pan Pacific Park. It is 5 miles from UCLA and within 11 miles of Santa Monica Pier and Los Angeles International Airport.

Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.
